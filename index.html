📚 Learn: Stock Market
📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a way of investing money in the stock market by putting in a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, no matter what the market is doing. This helps reduce the risk of investing all your money at the wrong time. It's like a steady, consistent savings plan, but for stocks. By doing this, you can avoid trying to time the market and just focus on investing regularly.

Think of it like: Imagine you're buying groceries every week, you don't stop buying because the prices are high one week, you just keep buying what you need at the current price, and over time, you'll average out the costs.
💬 Remember: Dollar cost averaging helps you invest consistently and avoid trying to time the market, which can lead to lower risk and more stable long-term returns.
