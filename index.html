📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a way to invest money in the stock market by putting in a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, no matter what the market is doing. This helps reduce the risk of investing all your money at the wrong time. It's like a steady, consistent approach to investing, rather than trying to time the market. By doing this, you can avoid making emotional decisions based on short-term market fluctuations.

Think of it like: Think of dollar cost averaging like filling up your gas tank at the same time every week, regardless of the price of gas. Sometimes you'll get a good deal, and sometimes you won't, but over time, you'll average out the cost and make sure you always have enough gas to get where you need to go.
💬 Remember: Dollar cost averaging helps you invest consistently and avoid making emotional decisions based on short-term market changes.
