📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a strategy where you invest a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, regardless of the market's current value. This way, you'll buy more shares when the market is low and fewer shares when the market is high, reducing the overall cost of your investment. By spreading out your investments over time, you'll minimize the impact of market fluctuations.

Think of it like: Imagine you're buying a bunch of apples at a grocery store. Instead of buying 10 apples at a high price one day, you buy 1-2 apples every week at a lower price, ensuring you get a good average price for all your apples.
💬 Remember: Invest a fixed amount regularly to reduce the impact of market fluctuations on your overall investment cost.
