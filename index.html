📚 Learn: Stock Market
📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a way to invest your money by putting in a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, no matter what the market is doing. This helps reduce the risk of investing a large sum of money all at once. It's like a steady, consistent savings plan, but for investing in the stock market. By doing this, you can avoid trying to time the market and make emotional decisions based on its ups and downs.

Think of it like: Imagine you're buying groceries every week, you don't stop buying because the prices are high one week, you just keep buying what you need and over time, you average out the costs. It's the same with dollar cost averaging, you keep investing a fixed amount regularly, regardless of the market's performance.
💬 Remember: Dollar cost averaging helps you invest consistently and avoid making emotional decisions based on market fluctuations, which can lead to a more stable and long-term investment strategy.
