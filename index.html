📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a way to invest your money by putting in a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, no matter what the market is doing. This helps reduce the risk of investing all your money at once when the market might be high. By doing this, you can avoid trying to time the market and just focus on investing regularly. It's a simple and steady approach to growing your wealth over time.

Think of it like: Think of dollar cost averaging like filling up your gas tank at the same time every week, regardless of the price of gas. Sometimes you'll get a good deal, and sometimes you won't, but over time you'll average out the cost and have a full tank.
💬 Remember: Investing a fixed amount of money at regular intervals can help you smooth out market ups and downs and build wealth over the long term.
