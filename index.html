📚 Learn: Stock Market
📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a way to invest your money by putting in a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, no matter what the market is doing. This helps reduce the risk of investing all your money at once, when the market might be high or low. By doing this, you can avoid trying to guess when the best time to invest is. It's a great way to make investing less scary and more consistent.

Think of it like: Think of it like buying groceries - you don't wait for the price of milk to go down before you buy it, you just buy it every week because you need it. Similarly, with dollar cost averaging, you invest a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, regardless of the market's ups and downs.
💬 Remember: Dollar cost averaging helps you invest consistently and reduce risk, by spreading out your investments over time, regardless of the market's performance.
