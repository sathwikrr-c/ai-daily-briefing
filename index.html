📚 Learn: Stock Market
📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a way to invest money in the stock market by putting in a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, no matter what the market is doing. This helps reduce the impact of market ups and downs on your investments. It's like a steady, long-term plan that helps you avoid trying to time the market. By doing this, you can invest without worrying about the best time to buy or sell.

Think of it like: Imagine you're buying groceries every week, you don't stop buying because the prices of some items went up, you continue to buy what you need at the current price, and over time, you average out the costs.
💬 Remember: Dollar cost averaging helps you invest consistently and reduce the risk of trying to time the market, making it a great strategy for long-term investors.
