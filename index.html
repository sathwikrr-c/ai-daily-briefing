📚 Learn: Stock Market
📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a way to invest money in the stock market by putting in a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, no matter what the market is doing. This helps reduce the risk of investing all your money at the wrong time. It's like a steady, consistent approach to investing, rather than trying to time the market perfectly. By doing this, you can avoid making emotional decisions based on market ups and downs.

Think of it like: Imagine you're buying groceries every week, you don't stop buying because the prices are high one week, you just keep buying the same amount every week, and over time, you average out the costs. It's similar with dollar cost averaging, you invest a fixed amount regularly, and over time, you average out the market's ups and downs.
💬 Remember: Dollar cost averaging helps you invest consistently and reduce risk by avoiding the attempt to time the market perfectly.
