📚 Learn: Stock Market
📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a way to invest your money by putting in a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, no matter what the market is doing. This helps reduce the risk of investing all your money at the wrong time. It's like a steady, long-term plan that helps you avoid making emotional decisions based on short-term market changes. By doing this, you can smooth out the ups and downs of the market and make investing less stressful.

Think of it like: Imagine you're buying groceries every week - you wouldn't stop buying food just because the prices went up one week, would you? You'd keep buying what you need, at the price it is, and that's kind of like what dollar cost averaging does for your investments.
💬 Remember: Dollar cost averaging helps you invest steadily and avoid making emotional decisions based on short-term market changes.
