📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a way to invest your money by putting in a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, no matter what the market is doing. This helps reduce the impact of market ups and downs on your investments. It's like a steady, consistent savings plan, but for investing in the stock market. By doing this, you can avoid trying to time the market and reduce your risk.

Think of it like: Think of dollar cost averaging like filling up your gas tank at the same time every week, regardless of whether gas prices are high or low. You're still putting in the same amount of money, but the amount of gas you get might vary.
💬 Remember: Dollar cost averaging helps you invest consistently and reduce risk, regardless of what's happening in the market.
