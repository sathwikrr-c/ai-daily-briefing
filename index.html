📚 Learn: Stock Market
📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a way to invest in the stock market where you put a fixed amount of money into the market at regular intervals, regardless of the current prices. This helps you reduce the impact of market fluctuations and timing risks. It's like setting aside a fixed amount each month for a savings goal, like buying a car or a house.

Think of it like: Think of it like buying a monthly subscription to your favorite streaming service, where you pay the same amount each month, whether the prices go up or down.
💬 Remember: Dollar cost averaging helps you invest consistently by reducing the impact of market ups and downs.

You're more likely to be influenced by the opinions of people who are similar to you.

This phenomenon is known as 'social influence through similarity.' Research has shown that when people are surrounded by those who share similar characteristics, values, or experiences, they are more likely to adopt the opinions and behaviors of those individuals. This can be seen in everything from fashion trends to political affiliations.
