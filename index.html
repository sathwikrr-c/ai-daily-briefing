📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a way to invest money in the stock market by putting in a fixed amount of money at regular times, no matter what the market is doing. This helps reduce the risk of investing all your money at the wrong time. It's like a steady, consistent approach to building wealth over time. By doing this, you can avoid trying to time the market, which can be very difficult.

Think of it like: Imagine you're buying groceries every week, but instead of trying to guess when the prices will be lowest, you just buy the same amount every week, no matter what. Sometimes you'll get a good deal, and sometimes you won't, but over time, you'll average out the costs.
💬 Remember: Dollar cost averaging helps you invest consistently and avoid trying to time the market, reducing your risk and increasing your potential for long-term success.
