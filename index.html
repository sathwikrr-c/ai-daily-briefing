📖 Today's Concept: Risk vs Return

When you invest in the stock market, you're taking a chance that you might make some money, but you also might lose some. The idea of risk vs return is that the more money you might make, the more likely you are to lose some too. Think of it like a game where you roll a dice - the higher the number you roll, the more money you might win, but the higher the number, the more likely you are to roll a 1 and lose. It's all about finding the right balance between how much you might win and how much you might lose.

Think of it like: Imagine you're choosing between a safe investment that earns 2% interest and a riskier investment that could earn 10% if you're lucky, but might also lose 20% if you're unlucky. It's like choosing between a steady, small raise at work and a chance at a big bonus that might not happen.
💬 Remember: The higher the potential return on an investment, the higher the risk of losing some or all of your money.
