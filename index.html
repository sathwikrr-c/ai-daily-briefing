📖 Today's Concept: Risk vs Return

When you invest in the stock market, you might get higher returns if you're willing to take more risk. However, taking too much risk can also lead to losing money. It's like a seesaw: the higher you go, the more likely you are to fall. You need to balance how much risk you're willing to take with how much reward you want.

Think of it like: Think of it like playing a game of surfing. If you surf on calm waters, you might not get a big thrill, but you won't wipe out either. If you surf on rough waters, you might catch a huge wave, but you could also get hurt.
💬 Remember: The amount of risk you take on in investing should be directly proportional to the potential returns you expect.
