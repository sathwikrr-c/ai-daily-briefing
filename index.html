📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a way to invest your money by putting in a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, no matter what the market is doing. This helps reduce the risk of investing all your money at the wrong time. It's like a steady, long-term plan that helps you smooth out the ups and downs of the market. By doing this, you can avoid trying to time the market and just focus on your long-term goals.

Think of it like: Think of it like filling up your gas tank - you put in a certain amount of gas every week, regardless of whether gas prices are high or low, to ensure you always have enough to get where you need to go.
💬 Remember: Dollar cost averaging helps you invest steadily and consistently, reducing the impact of market ups and downs on your investments.
