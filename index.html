📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a way of investing where you put a fixed amount of money into the stock market at regular intervals, no matter what the market is doing. This helps reduce the risk of investing a large sum of money at the wrong time. By investing a little bit of money regularly, you can avoid trying to time the market and make emotional decisions. Over time, this approach can help you build wealth steadily.

Think of it like: Think of dollar cost averaging like regularly adding a fixed amount of money to a piggy bank. Just as the piggy bank grows over time, your investments can grow too, even if the market has ups and downs.
💬 Remember: Investing a fixed amount of money at regular intervals can help you build wealth over time, regardless of market fluctuations.
