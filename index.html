📚 Learn: Stock Market
📖 Today's Concept: Dollar Cost Averaging

Dollar cost averaging is a way to invest money in the stock market by putting in a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, no matter what the market is doing. This helps reduce the risk of investing a large sum of money all at once. It's like a steady, consistent savings plan, but for investing in the stock market. By doing this, you can avoid trying to time the market and just focus on investing regularly.

Think of it like: Think of it like buying groceries - you don't try to buy all your food for the year at once, you buy a little every week. Similarly, dollar cost averaging helps you invest a little at a time, rather than all at once, to reduce the impact of market ups and downs.
💬 Remember: Investing a fixed amount of money at regular intervals can help reduce risk and make investing in the stock market more manageable.
